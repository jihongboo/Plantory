Plantory ("we", "our", or "us") provides plant identification, plant diagnosis, and plant care tools. This Privacy Policy explains what information Plantory may access, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, Plantory may process the following categories of information:

  • Plant photos that you take or import for plant recognition, diagnosis, or plant records.
  • Plant information you save, such as plant names, notes, care records, and diagnosis history.
  • Notification preferences you enable for plant care reminders.
  • Subscription and purchase status provided by Apple for premium features.
  • Basic technical data necessary to deliver app features and maintain service reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To identify plants and analyze visible plant issues from images you choose to submit.
  • To store and display your saved plants, records, and diagnosis history.
  • To sync your app data across supported Apple devices when iCloud or CloudKit-based sync is enabled.
  • To send local reminders if you choose to enable notifications.
  • To manage premium access and usage limits.
  • To improve app stability, security, and feature performance.

Photos and AI Processing

When you use AI-powered recognition or diagnosis features, the plant photo and related prompt data you choose to submit may be transmitted to our AI processing service so the requested analysis can be returned to you. Do not submit photos that contain sensitive personal information you do not want processed.

Cloud Sync

Plantory may use Apple CloudKit to sync your saved plant data across devices associated with your Apple account. Cloud-synced data is subject to Apple's infrastructure and policies.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Plantory may schedule local reminders on your device for plant care tasks. You can change notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

Purchases

Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only as needed to operate core app features, such as Apple services for storage and purchases, or service providers that help process AI requests on our behalf.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the app's features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Data stored on your device or in your cloud account may remain until you delete it.

Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to take or import photos.
  • You can edit or delete saved plant information in the app.
  • You can disable notifications in system settings.
  • You can manage subscriptions through your Apple account settings.

Children's Privacy

Plantory is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
